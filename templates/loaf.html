Ingredients

  • Strong white flour
    3 cups
  • Water
    1 cup
  • Yeast
    ¼ cup
  • Walnuts
    1 cup
  • Salt
    1 tsp

Timeline

  • 1 hr
    room

    Mix

    Mixed everything together and let the dough rest in the bowl.

  • 14 hr
    50°F

    Knead

    Kneaded the dough for 5 minutes and put it outside on the terrace to rest for 14 hours.

  • 1 hr
    room

    Knead

    A quick 3-minute knead.

  • 10 min
    room

    Shape dough

    Molded the dough into a baguette shape and made 4 diagonal cuts with a razor blade.

  • 30 min
    500°F

    Bake

    When the oven was ready, poured 3 cups of boiling water into a pan at the bottom of the oven to generate steam. Afterwards immediately slid the bread inside.
